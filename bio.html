Bio

Método Cicatriz is an artistic practice that explores the intersection between scientific observation and creative expression.

Through detailed studies of natural patterns and phenomena, this methodology seeks to understand the underlying structures that govern both artistic and scientific inquiry.

The work focuses on the documentation and interpretation of natural forms, creating a dialogue between empirical observation and aesthetic representation.
